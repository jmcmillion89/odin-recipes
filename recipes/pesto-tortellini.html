4-Ingredient Pesto Tortellini Bake

Ingredients

  • 1 (9 ounce) package tri-color cheese tortellini
  • 1 cup prepared pesto
  • 2 slices prosciutto, chopped
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese, divided

Instructions

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (180 degrees C).
  3. Fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a rolling boil; stir in tortellini and return to a boil.
  4. Cook uncovered, stirring occasionally, until tortellini float to the top and the filling is hot, about 3 minutes.
  5. Drain.
  6. Stir tortellini, prosciutto, pesto, and 1/2 cup mozzarella together in a large bowl.
  7. Pour into a baking dish. Sprinkle remaining mozzarella over the top.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven until cheese is melted and golden, about 10 minutes.
  9. Serve warm.
